Privacy Policy

Film Development Assistant

Effective Date: September 29, 2025

Your Privacy is Protected

Film Development Assistant does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data with the developer or third parties. Everything stays on your device.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. Film Development Assistant operates entirely on your device. All information you enter into the app (chemical batches, development sessions, notes, etc.) is stored locally on your device only.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using iOS's secure storage system. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export or backup your information through iOS's standard backup mechanisms.

Third-Party Services

Film Development Assistant does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No external servers are contacted during normal app usage.

App Store

This app is distributed through the Apple App Store. Apple may collect certain analytics and crash reporting data as part of their standard App Store operations, but this data is handled by Apple according to their privacy policy, not ours.

Updates to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices, we will update this policy and the app's version. However, our commitment to not collecting your data will remain unchanged.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact me at zack.t.burns@gmail.com or through the contact form.
